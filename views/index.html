NORTHPOINTE CHRISTIAN SCHOOL

A Christ-centered homeschool hybrid program for tomorrow's leader.

Biblical Curriculum

Alpha Omega is a Bible-based, award-winning homeschool curriculum that is fully accredited by the State of Texas. Scripture passages are integrated throughout worktexts to help students connect daily learning to biblical truth.

Community Oriented

Students are inside the classroom Tuesday-Thursday to receive instruction and build relationships with eachother. Because of the close-knit nature of our program, no student gets left out or behind. At NPCS, every student is seen, known, and celebrated.

Cost Effective

NPCS offers a Christian private school experience for only a fraction of the cost. To best fit your family's needs we offer three payment options for tuition: annual, semi-annual, and monthly.
